Welcome to St. Martin's Mabushi

Every Christian is called through Baptism to follow Jesus Christ by worshiping the Father in Sprit and in Truth, by loving one another, by proclaiming the Gospel, and by serving those in need. We hope this website will give you a glance of how we do these things here at St. Martin’s Catholic Church Mabushi. We live in a privileged moment in the history of the Church; this is the time of the New Evangelization, the time for us all to work towards the preaching the Gospel to all of Earth, reform the Church and renewing human civilization.

Thank you for coming to our Parish Website! Please explore and find out what we’re all about!
